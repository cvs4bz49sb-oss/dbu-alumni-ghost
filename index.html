Alumni Spotlight

Dr. Maria Gonzalez, '08

B.S. Biology · M.Ed. Educational Leadership · Class of 2008
"DBU didn't just prepare me for a career—it shaped me to serve. Every decision I make as a leader goes back to what I learned on that hill in Dallas."

Dr. Gonzalez now serves as Superintendent of a public school district in South Texas, where she has championed literacy programs reaching over 12,000 students. She credits DBU's emphasis on servant leadership as the foundation of her approach to education.
